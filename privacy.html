Privacy Policy

With this Privacy Policy I aim to inform you about how, why, and to what extent personal data is being processed by using this website and what your rights are as a Data Subject.

1. Data Controller

Unless otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy, the Data Controller in accordance with Art. 4 no. 7 GDPR is Joaquin Gomez Prats, Joachim-Karnatz-Allee 25, 10557 Berlin.

2. Accessing the website

When you use this website, the following information is being processed automatically:

• Information about the end device accessing the site and the software used
• Date and time of the access
• Websites from which the user has accessed our website or which the user accesses through our website
• IP address

The IP address must be processed for technical reasons in order to enable the website to be published to the user’s end device. The legal basis for data processing is provided by Art.6 para. 1 point b GDPR.

3. Contact initiated by you

When you contact me, for example using the phone or e-mail addresses, the data that you provide (your e-mail address, possibly your name and your telephone number) will be processed for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. The data collected in this connection will be deleted once it is no longer required for the aforementioned purpose, unless the deletion of this data would contravene statutory retention obligations. The legal basis for data processing is provided by Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 point f GDPR.

4. Your rights as Data Subject

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights with regard to your personal data:
- Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR),
- Right to rectification or to erasure (Art. 16, 17 GDPR),
- Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR),
- Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR),
- Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR).


You also have the right to submit a complaint to the competent supervisory authority and to seek legal remedies.
